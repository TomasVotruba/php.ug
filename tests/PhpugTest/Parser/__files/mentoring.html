Mentors and Apprentices

If you are interested in mentoring or being mentored, please add your name to the respective list below, including a short description of your goals in being a mentor or apprentice. Signing up puts you under no obligation to accept a mentor or apprentice; it simply gives us an indication of who might be interested.
If your email address is not available via your GitHub profile, please leave it here, or make yourself available in the IRC channel.

Once you have added yourself to the list, take a look through the list to see if anyone might be a good match for you. We'll try to match people up, but your best bet is to help yourself.

Format:
* <Real Name> (<Online Handle>, [github](https://github.com/<pagelink>)) - <Description of your goals in finding a mentor/apprentice>
